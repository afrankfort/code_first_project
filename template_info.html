Apple Cider Toner

Normal to Oily, Acne-prone skin

You need:

  • Filtered water
  • Lavender essential oil (optional)

Directions:

  1. For normal to dry skin, mix 50ml vinegar with 100ml filtered water. Add a few drops (5-10 drops) of Lavender oil.
  2. For oily skin, mix 75ml vinegar and 75ml filtered water. Add a few drops (I add 5-10 drop) of lavender oils.
  3. Store in a glass bottle at room temperature.

Rose Water Toner

Normal to dry skin

Rose water is a mild astringent so perfect for removing excess oil and to tighten pores to give a smooth and bright look to the skin.

You need:

  • 10 roses
  • Liquorice root
  • 2 Vitamin E (capsules) or 5 drops vegetable glycerine
  • Essential oils such as Tea tree, great for oily skin, and Witch hazel, tightens and smooths skin
  • Filtered water 500 ml
  • Glass bowl

Directions:

  1. Bring 500ml filtered water to the boil.
  2. Fill a glass bowl with boiling water
  3. Add rose petals and cover with a lid for 5 mins
  4. Add liquorice and cover with lid 15 mins
  5. Microwave 5 mins
  6. Mix and leave to cool to room temp
  7. Remove rose petals and liquorice
  8. Add 2 Vitamin capsules or 5 drops vegetable glycerine
  9. Add any 8 drops of your preferred essential oils

Easy to make and enjoy for a week!
