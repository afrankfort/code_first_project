How to Make a Change

The UN Sustainable Development Goals

The UN 17 SDGs focus on issues surrounding equality, the environment, workers' rights, industry and justice to name a few. These goals are intended to ensure a positive and sustainable future for all by alleviating inequality, poverty, water poverty and loss of habitats for humans and animals. These goals aim to be met by 2030 to ensure peace and a prosperous future for people all over the globe. We wanted to pick a few that can help you in your day to day efforts related to sustainability, however we encourage you to read more about the fantastic projects that tackle issues on education, equality, energy and health.

12 tips to save on resources and money

  2. Use a bowl for washing up

  5. Fit a water efficient shower head
  6. Spend 1 min less in your daily shower
  7. Draught proof doors and windows
  8. Install chimney draught excluder
  9. Use smart heating controls
  10. Turn your thermostat down 1 degree
  11. Replace all bulbs with LEDs
  12. Turn off your lights
