Beauty

A great way to reduce plastics use is by making homemade products for your beauty regime and the home.

Apple Cider Toner

Normal to Oily, Acne-prone skin

You need:

  • Filtered water
  • Lavender essential oil (optional)

Directions:

  1. For normal to dry skin, mix 50ml vinegar with 100ml filtered water. Add a few drops (5-10 drops) of Lavender oil.
  2. For oily skin, mix 75ml vinegar and 75ml filtered water. Add a few drops (I add 5-10 drop) of lavender oils.
  3. Store in a glass bottle at room temperature.

Rose water Toner

Normal to Oily

Rose water is a mild astringent so perfect for removing excess oil and to tighten pores to give a smooth and bright look to the skin.

You need:

  • 10 roses
  • Liquorice root
  • 2 Vitamin E (capsules) or 5 drops vegetable glycerine
  • Essential oils such as Tea tree, great for oily skin, and Witch hazel, tightens and smooths skin
  • Filtered water 500 ml
  • Glass bowl

Directions:

  1. Bring 500ml filtered water to the boil.
  2. Fill a glass bowl with boiling water
  3. Add rose petals and cover with a lid for 5 mins
  4. Add liquorice and cover with lid 15 mins
  5. Microwave 5 mins
  6. Mix and leave to cool to room temp
  7. Remove rose petals and liquorice
  8. Add 2 Vitamin capsules or 5 drops vegetable glycerine
  9. Add any 8 drops of your preferred essential oils

Easy to make and enjoy for a week!

Shaving Cream

You need:

  • 76g shea butter
  • 55g sweet almond oil
  • 10 drops rosemary essential oil
  • 3-5 drops peppermint essential oil

Directions:

  1. Over low heat combine the shea butter and coconut oil in a small saucepan. Stir until just melted. Remove from the heat and transfer to a glass bowl. Add in sweet almond oil and essential oils. Stir to mix.

  2. Place the bowl in the refrigerator and chill. When solid whip using a hand beater or electric mixer until light and fluffy. Scoop into a jar with a lid and keep in a cool, dry place.

Air freshener

Easy to make home spray to customize with your favourite scents

You need:

  • 11 tablespoon witch hazel (or isopropyl alcohol)
  • 30 drops essential oil
  • 1/2 cup distilled water

Directions:

  1. Add witch hazel or rubbing alcohol to the spray bottle.
  2. Add your essential oils of choice.
  3. Swirl the bottle about 10 times to combine oils with witch hazel.
  4. Top off with water, leaving a little room at the top for shaking.
